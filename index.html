About MOTJHOLOKO ECD

Welcome to MOTJHOLOKO ECD, where we provide a safe, secure, and nurturing environment for children aged 6 months to 5 years. Our facility is accredited, with a health & safety, ensuring a clean, neat, and stimulating space for early learning and development.

Under the leadership of Principal Mosebi Mannini, we are committed to building bright futures, one child at a time. Our center is located at 1100 Molele Street, West Jabavu, Soweto, offering convenient access for families in the community.

We believe in holistic development that nurtures each child's cognitive, emotional, social, and physical growth through carefully designed activities and a loving environment.

Outdoor Play

Social Development
